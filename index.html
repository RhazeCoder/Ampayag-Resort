About US

Resort Overview

Welcome to Ampayag Resort, a luxurious oasis nestled in the heart of nature. Established in 2015, Ampayag Resort was created to provide an escape from the fast pace of modern life, blending comfort, adventure, and sustainability. With a mission to deliver unforgettable experiences, our resort offers world-class accommodations, locally inspired dining, and a range of activities that showcase the beauty of our unique location. At Ampayag Resort, we pride ourselves on combining the warmth of Filipino hospitality with world-class facilities, ensuring every guest leaves with cherished memories. Come and experience Ilocos like never before—Ampayag Resort awaits you.

Resort Vision

Our vision is to create a space where guests can unwind, reconnect with themselves and nature, and leave with cherished memories. Surrounded by lush landscapes and crystal-clear waters, Ampayag Resort is committed to preserving the environment while delivering unmatched hospitality.
